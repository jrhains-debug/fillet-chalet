## Brand Identity
- Mobile wood-fired sauna business in Portland, Maine
- Tone: Unpretentious, social, authentic (NOT spa/wellness vibes)
- Think: Russian & Turkish Baths NYC, grandpa's fishing shack, old camp Maine
- Target: Private rentals for groups, occasional pop-ups

## Physical Sauna Details
- Built from old ice fishing shack on trailer
- Exterior: Sheet metal, small windows, wooden fish sign
- Interior: Hemlock or cedar, Nippa wood stove (Michigan, since 1930)
- Owner: Jeffrey, Portland bartender, no prior building experience

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 14+ with App Router
- Tailwind CSS
- Pages: Home, About, Services, Gallery, Contact
